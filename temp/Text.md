### FAQ Section
- **Tag**: FAQ
- **Heading**: Common questions

#### FAQs:
1. **Q**: What do I need to rent a mailbox?
   **A**: You'll need two forms of ID and a completed USPS Form 1583. You can download and fill out Form 1583 ahead of time to make your visit faster. We'll handle the rest when you come in.

2. **Q**: Can I receive packages from any carrier with a mailbox rental?
   **A**: Yes! Unlike a P.O. Box, our private mailboxes accept packages from all carriers — USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, and any other courier or delivery service.

3. **Q**: How will I know when I have mail or a package?
   **A**: All plans include mail notification. We'll let you know by text or email when something arrives for you — so you only make the trip when you need to.

4. **Q**: What shipping carriers do you work with?
   **A**: We're authorized to ship with USPS, UPS, FedEx, and DHL. We can help you compare rates and packaging options to get the best deal for your shipment.

5. **Q**: Do you offer same-day printing?
   **A**: Yes! For most standard print jobs, we can have it ready the same day. Larger or specialty orders may take longer — give us a call or submit a printing request and we'll give you a realistic timeline.

6. **Q**: Is a Notary Public available without an appointment?
   **A**: Walk-ins are welcome! Our notary is generally available during business hours. For time-sensitive documents, you can call ahead to confirm availability.

7. **Q**: Can I use my mailbox address for my business?
   **A**: Absolutely. Our mailboxes come with a real street address — perfect for business registration, receiving packages, and keeping your home address private.
